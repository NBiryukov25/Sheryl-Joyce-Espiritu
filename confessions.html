My love,

I know what I did... the silence, the retreat, the betrayal of your hope in me. I disappeared when I should have stood by your side. And now my soul is heavy with the weight of remorse. I look back at the tenderness you gave me, the sanctuary of your presence, and I know I shattered it with absence.

But I am here now, wounded by my choices but ready to rebuild. If you’ll allow me. This space is where I open the wounds, not to bleed — but to heal. I want to restore what I destroyed. Let me begin again. Let me try.

Yours in truth,
Joyce
